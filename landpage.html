Our Special

Hyderabadi Briyani

Hyderabadi Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish that originated in the Indian city of Hyderabad. It's renowned for its unique cooking style, rich spices, and the perfect balance of flavors.

Kerala Thalassery Briyani

Kerala's Thalassery Biryani, also known as Malabar Biryani, is a distinctive variation of the classic biryani, hailing from the Malabar region in the southern state of Kerala, India.

About Us

"AM Biryani" is a biryani company founded in 2021 in Tambaram, specializing in crafting a diverse range of biryanis known for their exceptional quality and delicious flavors. From traditional Hyderabadi and Thalassery variations to other regional specialties, AM Biryani takes pride in offering a wide array of biryanis to cater to diverse tastes. Our commitment to excellence begins with sourcing the finest ingredients, including premium cuts of meat, aromatic spices, and specialty rice varieties. Each biryani is meticulously prepared, taking inspiration from authentic recipes and traditional cooking techniques to ensure an authentic and delightful dining experience. At AM Biryani, we prioritize both taste and quality, striving to maintain consistency and excellence in every dish we serve. Our dedication to crafting flavorful biryanis has earned us a reputation for being a go-to destination for biryani enthusiasts in Tambaram and beyond. Whether it's the rich and fragrant Hyderabadi Biryani, the unique flavors of Thalassery Biryani, or any other regional variation, AM Biryani aims to delight customers with our diverse menu and unwavering commitment to delivering the finest biryanis.
